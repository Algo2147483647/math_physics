# Abelian Variety

[TOC]

## Define

> An abelian variety is a complete algebraic group variety, central in arithmetic geometry.

An **abelian variety** over a field $K$ is a complete, connected algebraic group over $K$. Equivalently in classical language, it is a projective algebraic variety equipped with a compatible commutative group law.

Abelian varieties generalize elliptic curves to higher dimension and are basic objects in arithmetic geometry.

## Properties

### Dimension One Case

An elliptic curve over a field is an abelian variety of dimension $1$.

### Arithmetic Role

Abelian varieties carry rich structures such as torsion points, Galois actions on Tate modules, and height pairings.
