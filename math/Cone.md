# Cone

[TOC]

## Define

> A cone is a subset closed under multiplication by positive scalars.

A cone in a real vector space $V$ is a subset $C \subseteq V$ such that
$$
\forall \boldsymbol x \in C,\ \forall \theta > 0,
\quad \theta \boldsymbol x \in C.
$$

A cone records rays emanating from the origin and is naturally studied as a subset of a linear space.

## Properties

### Convex Cone

A convex cone is a cone closed under addition, equivalently under nonnegative linear combinations:
$$
\forall \boldsymbol x_1, \boldsymbol x_2 \in C,\ \forall \theta_1, \theta_2 \ge 0,
\quad \theta_1 \boldsymbol x_1 + \theta_2 \boldsymbol x_2 \in C.
$$
