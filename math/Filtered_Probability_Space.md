## Define

> A filtered probability space is a probability space equipped with an increasing family of sigma-algebras representing information over time.

$$
(\Omega, \mathcal F, (\mathcal F_i)_{i \in I}, P)  \tag{Filtered Probability Space}
$$

Filtered Probability Space is a probability space equipped with a filtration.

$$
(\mathcal F_i)_{i \in I}  \tag{Filtration}
$$

A filtration $(\mathcal F_i)_{i \in I}$ is a sequence of $\sigma$-algebras in a probability space $(\Omega, \mathcal F, P)$ and an index set $I$ with a total order $\le$, such that
$$
\mathcal F_i \subseteq \mathcal F_j \quad, \forall i \le j
$$
