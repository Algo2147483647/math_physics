# Galois Representation

[TOC]

## Define

> A Galois representation is a homomorphism from a Galois group into a linear group over a coefficient field.

A **Galois representation** is typically a continuous homomorphism
$$
ho : \operatorname{Gal}(\overline K / K) 	o \operatorname{GL}_n(E),
$$
where $K$ is a field and $E$ is a coefficient field such as a finite extension of $\mathbb Q_p$. Galois representations are central objects in modern arithmetic geometry.

## Properties

### Arithmetic Role

Galois representations encode arithmetic information coming from elliptic curves, modular forms, and etale cohomology.

### Typical Coefficient Fields

Common choices of coefficient field include finite extensions of $\mathbb Q_p$, finite fields, and sometimes $\mathbb C$.
