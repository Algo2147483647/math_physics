# Lie Group

[TOC]

## Define

A real Lie group is a [group](./Group.md) that is also a finite-dimensional real smooth [manifold](./Manifold.md), in which the group operations of multiplication and inversion are smooth maps.

$$
\mu: G \times G \to G, \quad \mu(x,y) = xy
$$

## Properties

### Lie Algebra

Associated to a Lie group is its Lie algebra, which can be identified with the tangent space at the identity together with the Lie bracket induced by the group structure.
