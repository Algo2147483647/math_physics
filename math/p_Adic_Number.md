# p Adic Number

[TOC]

## Define

> The $p$-adic numbers form the basic characteristic-zero local field attached to a prime $p$.

For a prime number $p$, the **field of $p$-adic numbers** is denoted by
$$
\mathbb Q_p.
$$
It is obtained as the completion of $\mathbb Q$ with respect to the $p$-adic absolute value.

## Properties

### Construction

The field $\mathbb Q_p$ is the completion of $\mathbb Q$ under the norm determined by the $p$-adic valuation.

### Arithmetic Role

The $p$-adic numbers are the basic local objects for studying congruences, local Galois theory, and local-global principles.
