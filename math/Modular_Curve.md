# Modular Curve

[TOC]

## Define

> A modular curve is an algebraic curve that parametrizes elliptic curves equipped with level structure.

A **modular curve** is an algebraic curve arising as a quotient of the upper half-plane by a congruence subgroup, together with its natural compactification. It plays a central role in the interaction between elliptic curves, modular forms, and Galois representations.

## Properties

### Moduli Interpretation

Classically, modular curves parametrize elliptic curves together with additional level structure.

### Arithmetic Role

They provide the geometric setting for many modularity statements in arithmetic geometry.
