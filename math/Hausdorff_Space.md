[TOC]

## Define

Hausdorff Space $X$ is a topology space where, for any distinct points $x, y \in X$, there exist a neighborhood $U$ of $x$ and a neighborhood $V$ of $y$ such that $U$ and $V$ are disjoint.

$$
U \cap V = \emptyset
$$
