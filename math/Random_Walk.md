# Random Walk

[TOC]

## Define

> A random walk is the partial-sum process of independent random increments.

A **random walk** is a stochastic process $(S_n)_{n \ge 0}$ of the form
$$
S_0 = 0,
\qquad
S_n = X_1 + \cdots + X_n,
$$
where $(X_n)$ are independent and identically distributed increments.

## Properties

### Simple Random Walk

In one dimension, the simple random walk has increments taking values $\pm 1$.

### Scaling Limit

Under diffusive rescaling, random walks converge in distribution to Brownian motion under standard hypotheses.
